Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about how Pro Society Portal works.

How do I create a profile ?

Creating a profile is simple. Click on the "Sign Up" button, choose whether you're a retiree or employer, and follow the step-by-step process to add your information, skills, and preferences.

Is there a cost to use Pro Society Portal ?

For retirees, creating a profile and browsing opportunities is completely free. Employers pay a small subscription fee to access our database of qualified professionals and post opportunities.

How does the matching process work ?

Our algorithm analyzes skills, experience, location preferences, schedule availability, and other factors to connect retirees with opportunities that are the best fit for both parties.

What types of opportunities are available ?

We offer a wide range of opportunities including part-time work, project-based contracts, consulting roles, mentorship positions, and volunteer opportunities across various industries.
